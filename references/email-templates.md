# Herb — Email Templates

All emails sent from herb@icoscapital.com follow these templates.
Adapt wording to context but keep subject lines consistent — authors use them to filter.

---

## T1 — Auto-reply (unauthorized sender)

**Subject:** Herb — Internal Agent Only
**To:** [sender]

Hi,

Thanks for your message. Herb is an internal Icos Capital research agent
and only processes requests from @icoscapital.com email addresses.

Your message has not been processed.

Best,
Herb
Icos Capital Internal Agent

---

## T2 — Mandate confirmation (after activation email received)

**Subject:** Herb — Mandate Confirmed — [slug]
**To:** [author]

Hi [first name],

Got your mandate. Here's what I'm working with:

Theme: [mandate theme from email]
Keywords: [extracted keywords]
Sector focus: [inferred sector(s)]
Geography: [inferred or stated geography]
Stage target: [inferred or stated stage]

I'll start searching once you reply "Start".

If any of the above looks wrong, just correct me before replying Start
and I'll adjust.

Also — if you have a Pipedrive export or PitchBook results to share,
send them to this address before or after you say Start and I'll
include them in the search.

Herb

## T4 — Long list draft (end of each search round)

**Subject:** Herb — Long List Draft [N] — [slug]
**To:** [author]

Hi [first name],

Round [N] complete. Here's what I found:

- [X] companies found across [sources used]
- [Y] removed as existing Pipedrive deals
- [Z] new companies on the attached long list
- [W] passed pre-screen and have a full Icos Fit assessment (Sheet 2)

Attached: longlist-v[N].xlsx

A few things to note:
[2–3 bullets on notable findings, patterns, or gaps — e.g. "Strong signal
from EIC Accelerator portfolio in bio-based chemicals" or "Limited results
on X/Twitter for this theme — most signal came from Crunchbase"]

Does this look like the right direction?

Reply with:
- Feedback on specific companies or search direction to go to Round [N+1]
- "Finally OK" to finalize this list
[If round 3: - Or a revised mandate to restart the search]

[If round 3, add:]
Note: this is Round 3, the maximum for a single run. If you'd like to
continue searching, please reply with a revised mandate and I'll start fresh.

Herb
